media paintings

2011

These works represent an early interest and extending the surface of a painting into the physical space of the viewer. The pictorial realm of the canvas is reliant on movement or illumination from video, light, and sound. To build on that three-dimensionality, sewing or printing techniques call further attention to the physicality of the surface.
