pin work

2011-2012

Needle and thread and ballpoint pins are very physical lines. In thinking about breaking the physical plane of the illusory canvas, these material become a great means to bring the line from the front to the back of the piece. These materials being strongly associated with domestic craft, make light of a male-centric history in painting
