Nostalgia Index

2014

In this series, a modified version of the NASA insignia is embroidered on to discarded, brittle endsheets from books that have been repaired in the Preservation Lab I work in by day. Each drawing is given an indexical title: NASA(thread make)(thread #)(number) i.e. NASADMC82502.
